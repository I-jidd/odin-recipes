Adobo

Description

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish known for its rich and savory flavors. It typically consists of meat (often chicken or pork) marinated and simmered in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. The dish is then cooked until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon fresh-ground black pepper
  • 2 1/2 pounds lean pork, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 pound small green beans

How to cook Pork Adobo Step-by-step

  1. Marinate the pork
  2. Cook the pork
  3. Simmer the Adobo
  4. Adjust seasoning
  5. serve
